Gaia helps children aged 4–10 build emotional responsibility by caring for a real plant. The child is the caregiver — not an app, not a timer. Here's how it works.

How it works
The character tells the child what the plant needs. The child decides what to do. That moment — noticing, deciding, acting — is where the emotional development happens. No automation. No self-watering. The child is the caregiver.
Worth clarifying: Gaia does not come with a plant, and the character is not a robot. The plant is yours — any plant you already have at home. The character is a physical toy that reacts to the plant's condition through the sensor.
