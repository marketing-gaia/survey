How do your kids
connect with nature?
We're building something for families like yours. Tell us about your child and we'll build it right.

Gaia is a companion toy for children aged 4–10 that connects them to a real plant. A sensor in the soil tracks the plant's health. A companion toy the child carries around changes expression when the plant needs attention. The child decides what to do. That's it.

How it works
The character tells the child what the plant needs. The child decides what to do. That moment — noticing, deciding, acting — is where the emotional development happens. No automation. No self-watering. The child is the caregiver.
Good to know: The plant is yours — anything already at home works. The character is a physical toy, not a screen. The sensor connects the two.
